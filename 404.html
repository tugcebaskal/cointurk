CRYPTO CURRENCY

Cryptocurrency is a digital payment system that does not use banks to verify transactions. It is a peer-to-peer system that allows anyone in any location to send and receive payments. Instead of physical money being carried and exchanged in the real world, crypto payments exist solely as digital entries in an online database that identify specific transactions. When you transfer cryptocurrency funds, the transactions are recorded in a public ledger. Cryptocurrencies are stored in digital wallets.
